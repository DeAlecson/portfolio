KyoCards Marketplace

Problem. A trading-card marketplace for Southeast Asia needed to go from nothing to live in 4 months. Budget was fixed and the launch date was tied to a trade show booth we'd already paid for, so it wasn't really moveable.

Approach. Owned delivery end-to-end as Lead PM and Product Owner. PRDs, acceptance criteria, the full Jira sprint cycle with 8 engineers. The slower part of the week was usually the scope conversations with co-founders and funders, especially when a new feature got pushed in mid-sprint and something had to give.

The trade-off in the end: international shipping APIs and automated KYC got deferred to a post-MVP roll-up. Both shipped roughly 8 weeks after launch, with full stakeholder sign-off.

Outcome. MVP shipped on time. By the end of Year 1 the platform had cleared 10,000 registered users.
